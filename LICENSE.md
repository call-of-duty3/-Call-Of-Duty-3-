## 📖 About Call of Duty 3

**Call of Duty 3** is a critically acclaimed World War II first-person shooter developed by Treyarch and published by Activision. Set during the Normandy Breakout campaign, the game follows multiple Allied soldiers as they engage in fierce combat against Axis forces across occupied France.

Unlike previous entries, Call of Duty 3 presents the war from several perspectives, allowing players to experience the battlefield through the eyes of American, British, Canadian, and Polish soldiers.

Featuring cinematic storytelling, realistic environments, and large-scale battles, Call of Duty 3 remains a memorable title in the legendary Call of Duty franchise.

## 🕹️ Gameplay Overview

In **Call of Duty 3**, players participate in a variety of missions inspired by historical events during the final stages of World War II.

Gameplay includes:

* Assaulting enemy strongholds.
* Defending strategic positions.
* Escorting military convoys.
* Operating tanks and military vehicles.
* Coordinating with squad members during combat.
* Completing objectives under intense battlefield conditions.

The game combines fast-paced action with tactical gameplay, creating an immersive war experience.
